# Glossary

## Scope

This glossary defines terminology used throughout ununseptium documentation.

### Non-Goals

- Comprehensive regulatory definitions (consult official sources)
- Legal definitions (consult legal professionals)

## Terms

### A

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| ADWIN | Adaptive Windowing - algorithm for detecting concept drift |
| AML | Anti-Money Laundering - regulations and practices to prevent money laundering |
| AUC | Area Under the Curve - model performance metric |
| Audit Log | Chronological record of system activities |

### B

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Beneficial Owner | Natural person who ultimately owns or controls an entity |
| BSA | Bank Secrecy Act - US AML legislation |

### C

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| CDD | Customer Due Diligence - process of verifying customer identity and risk |
| CFT | Combating the Financing of Terrorism |
| Conformal Prediction | Framework for prediction sets with coverage guarantees |
| Copula | Function describing dependency structure between random variables |
| CTR | Currency Transaction Report - mandatory filing for large transactions |
| CUSUM | Cumulative Sum - sequential change detection algorithm |

### D

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Data Drift | Change in input data distribution over time |
| Due Diligence | Investigation before establishing a business relationship |

### E

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| EDD | Enhanced Due Diligence - additional checks for high-risk customers |
| EVT | Extreme Value Theory - statistics of rare events |

### F

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| FATF | Financial Action Task Force - intergovernmental AML/CFT body |
| False Positive | Incorrect alert on legitimate activity |
| Fernet | Symmetric encryption scheme based on AES-128-CBC |

### G

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| GPD | Generalized Pareto Distribution - EVT distribution for exceedances |
| Graph Motif | Small recurring subgraph pattern |

### H

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Hash Chain | Sequence of hashes where each depends on its predecessor |
| Hawkes Process | Self-exciting point process for event clustering |

### I

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Identity Verification | Process of confirming a persons claimed identity |

### K

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| KYC | Know Your Customer - identity verification and risk assessment |

### L

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Layering | AML typology - moving funds to disguise origin |

### M

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Model Card | Structured documentation of ML model characteristics |
| Model Drift | Degradation of model performance over time |

### N

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Neural ODE | Neural network defining continuous-time dynamics |

### P

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| PEP | Politically Exposed Person - individual in prominent public position |
| PII | Personally Identifiable Information |
| PINN | Physics-Informed Neural Network |
| Placement | AML typology - introducing illicit funds into financial system |
| PSI | Population Stability Index - distribution shift metric |

### R

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| RBA | Risk-Based Approach - allocating resources based on risk |
| RBAC | Role-Based Access Control |
| ROC | Receiver Operating Characteristic - model performance curve |

### S

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| SAR | Suspicious Activity Report - regulatory filing |
| SHAP | SHapley Additive exPlanations - model interpretability method |
| SPRT | Sequential Probability Ratio Test - hypothesis testing |
| Structuring | AML typology - breaking transactions to avoid reporting thresholds |

### T

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Tamper-Evident | Property indicating whether data has been modified |
| Typology | Pattern or method of money laundering |

### U

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| Uncertainty Quantification | Methods for estimating prediction confidence |

### V

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| VaR | Value at Risk - quantile-based risk measure |
